Turn based (collectible) strategy card game, set in Green universe. 

Format: Android Device

Each player starts with one pre chosen hero unit, and one resource card.
Every turn, resource cards produce resources that can be used to play additional units, items(?) 
equipment or abilities. It can also be saved.

Heroes are special units that have additional stats and special abilities. If a hero is slain,
the player looses the game.
